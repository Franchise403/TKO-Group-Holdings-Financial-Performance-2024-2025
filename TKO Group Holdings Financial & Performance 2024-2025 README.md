\# TKO Group Holdings Financial \& Performance 2024-2025



This project contains quarterly financial and operational performance datasets for TKO Group Holdings, UFC, and WWE.



The datasets were designed for business intelligence and analytics workflows using Power BI, SQL Server, or other reporting platforms. Metrics include revenue performance, revenue stream segmentation, attendance trends, ticket distribution estimates, and event activity across UFC and WWE brands.



\## Project Objectives



\- Analyze quarterly TKO financial performance

\- Compare UFC and WWE revenue trends

\- Track revenue stream composition across media rights, sponsorships, live events, and consumer products

\- Measure attendance and ticket distribution trends

\- Create operational KPIs for event activity

\- Support dashboard development in Power BI



\## Dataset Structure



\### TKOPerformance.csv

Quarter-level consolidated TKO financial performance and business segment revenue metrics.



\### UFCPerformance.csv

Quarter-level UFC revenue, attendance, and ticketed event metrics.



\### WWEPerformance.csv

Quarter-level WWE revenue, estimated ticket distribution, and ticketed event metrics.



\## Data Model



Grain: 1 row = 1 fiscal quarter



The datasets are structured for analytical modeling and reporting workflows. Revenue metrics are stored in millions of USD and operational metrics are aggregated to the quarterly level.

\## Data Limitations



\- WWE ticket distribution figures are estimated using WrestleTix reporting.

\- UFC attendance figures are based on announced attendance.

\- Some TKO business segment revenues were not separately disclosed during portions of 2024 and therefore contain NULL values.

\- This dataset is intended for analytical and educational purposes.

TKO Financials & UFC/WWE Performance – Data Dictionary

Purpose:
Contains quarterly financial and operational performance metrics for TKO Group Holdings, UFC, and WWE. The dataset is designed for business intelligence analysis, including revenue trends, revenue stream comparison, event activity, attendance, and ticket distribution.

Grain: 1 row = 1 fiscal quarter

Notes:
-Financial columns ending in _M are stored in millions of USD.
-Revenue stream columns allow comparison across media rights, live events, sponsorship, and consumer products.
-UFC attendance values use announced attendance figures.
-WWE ticket distribution values are estimated and should be treated as directional rather than official audited totals.
-Ticketed event counts can be used to calculate average attendance or estimated tickets distributed per event.
-QuarterStartDate and QuarterEndDate support time-based filtering and date table relationships in Power BI or SQL.
-QuarterNumber can be used for proper quarter sorting in reports and dashboards.
-IMG_TotalRevenue_M and Other_TotalRevenue_M contain NULL values for portions of 2024 because these business segment revenues were not separately reported in available TKO financial disclosures during that reporting period.


References:
-TKO financial data was collected from TKO Group Holdings quarterly financial reports available through the TKO Investor Relations website: https://investor.tkogrp.com/overview/default.aspx
-WWE estimated ticket distribution data was collected from Wrestlenomics WrestleTix reports: https://wrestlenomics.com/wrestletix
-UFC attendance data was collected from individual UFC event pages available on Wikipedia.

Data Source Notes:
-Financial metrics are reported as disclosed in official TKO quarterly earnings reports.
-WWE ticket distribution figures are estimates compiled from WrestleTix reporting and should not be considered official WWE attendance records.
-UFC attendance figures are based on announced attendance reported for individual events and aggregated to the fiscal quarter level.
